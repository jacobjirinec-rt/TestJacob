A Poem for Noah

Noah, your name like the ark of old,
Carries a story waiting to be told,
Of courage that weathered the fiercest rain,
And faith that bloomed through every pain.

Like waters that receded to reveal new ground,
In you, a steady strength is found,
A heart that holds both calm and storm,
A spirit gentle, kind, and warm.

May your days be filled with skies of blue,
And every dream you chase come true,
For in this world of ebb and flow,
You are the light that makes it glow.

With love and warmth, this verse is penned,
For Noah — a name that will never end.
